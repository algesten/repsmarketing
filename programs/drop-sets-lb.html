Drop Dead Drop Sets

By allowing you to specify rest intervals, Reps & Sets let you specify drop sets in a program. Drop sets provide a very intensive way of overloading your muscles. When you reach failure in a set, rather than resting, you drop the weight and continue until you reach failure again - usually repeating three times. When working with barbells, these are sometimes called strip sets because you strip the bar of plates as you go.
