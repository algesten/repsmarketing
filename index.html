A better way to log your workouts.

Pre-program your regular workouts, and then simply check off each set as you go. No need to keep entering reps and weights. It's so simple, you'll wonder why all fitness apps don't work this way.

Select weights visually.

Making logging fun and intuitive.

When you include rest intervals in a program, Reps & Sets handles the timers for you. Just check off a set and the next rest timer starts automatically, pre-set to the rest interval you specified in your program.
