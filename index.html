Create workout to-do lists.

A better way to log your workouts.

Pre-program your regular workouts, and then simply check off each set as you go. No need to keep entering reps and weights. It's so simple, you'll wonder why all fitness apps don't work this way.

See workout to-dos in action

Select weights visually.

Making logging fun and intuitive.

Dumbbells, kettlebells, barbells, medicine balls, machines... Reps & Sets knows what equipment you’re using, so weight selectors in the app look exactly like your real weights in the gym.

See visual weight selectors in action

Trigger automatic rest-timers.

So you can spend your rest time resting.

When you include rest intervals in a program, Reps & Sets handles the timers for you. Just check off a set and the next rest timer starts automatically, pre-set to the rest interval you specified in your program.

See automatic rest-timers in action

Illustrated exercise manual.

We show you what other apps only tell you.

Our built-in exercise manual features hundreds of illustrations and interactive diagrams that provide a handy visual reference to what each muscle does in an exercise. All optimised for a compact 13 MB storage.

See the exercise manual in action

Track your progress.

See exactly how you’ve been doing.

In the history tab you can chart your progress with visual graphs for each exercise; browse your history in calendar view; check your estimated one-rep-max; or export a CSV file and get clever with spreadsheets.

See history and data export in action
